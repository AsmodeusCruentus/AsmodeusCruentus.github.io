Cradle of Fear

  • Alex Chandon - Writer & Director
  • Dani Filth - Lead Role

Cradle of Fear is a1 British horror film Directed by Alex Chandon4. He took great inspiration from the Anthology films that were produced by the film studio Amicus Productions5 around the 70s. The Amicus Production produced films like From beyond the grave & Tales from the Crypt & Asylum. The Films runtime is around 120 minutes and consists of three 30 minute stories that are then tied together by a fourth overall story.

The plot of the movie focuses around an serial killer named Kemper, After being improsed in an insane asylum he starts causing mayhem for those at fault for his imprisonment. Kemper was the son of an infamous satanist and used hypnosis to carry out his crimes. The main character is "The Man" who is protrayed by Dani Filth2 who finds the perputrators of Kempers capture and finds ways for them to get into accidents and die, all of which sparks the interested of Inspector Peter Neilsson. All these events remind Neilson of a serial killer he has imprisoned years ago.
